The heritage of Sweet Flavor

In 1936, an icon was born, Honey. Since then, the brand has grown into the most natural and original soft drink in many countries worldwide. Natural, because of its unique recipe including orange pulp. Original, thanks to its iconic bulby bottle, the ritual of shaking and its witty advertising campaigns.
